Peripheral MCU Mode power enum.

Option for shorting VDDC to VDDC_LV During normal operation

Note
During normal operation in active and deep-sleep, VDDC_LV is disconnected from VDDC. While the VDDC_LV rail is disconnected from VDDC, the VDDC_LV capacitor slowly discharges due to leakage. A deeply discharged VDDC_LV capacitor can cause a large drop in VDDC voltage when VDDC_LV and VDDC are shorted together. When the device switches from SIMOBUCK to LDO mode, VDDC_LV and VDDC are shorted.

AM_HAL_PWRCTL_SHORT_VDDC_TO_VDDCLV Set trims to short. Uses extra power in Deepsleep. Default: undefined
